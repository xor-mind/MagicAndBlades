Game Overview: UO(highly simplified) meets Zelda meets Gauntlet.

You run around killing monsters, leveling up your skills, collecting treasure all in the hopes of beating the bad guy at the end of the level.

The leveling up system is so you can defeat tougher monsters, using your skills on them levels you up.

You know how hard a monster is by the difficulty flag, grey, yellow, red.

You can either be a warrior or a mage. There will only be one skill for each type. Warrior is swords, mage is magic. As you level up, your weapons and spell effects change color.
